2. Making web analytics work for you
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Think about the big-picture goals for your business. Are you trying to maximise revenue? To improve margins? To cut costs? How do your website and your online marketing campaigns help you achieve those goals? Write down your thoughts – this will help focus your efforts with analytics.
