1. Introduction for advertising on mobile
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Practise writing clear, concise ads that will appeal to mobile users on the go. What do you need to emphasise? What are some calls to action you can use?




------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. Search campaigns for mobile
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Visit your website from a mobile device. Are your landing pages optimised for mobile? Are your calls to action front and centre? Are text and buttons large so visitors can easily read them? If not, what changes should you make to your mobile site?




------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. Display campaigns for mobile
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Read about how to use Google's Display Planner to find ways to target your audience on the Display Network: https://www.thinkwithgoogle.com/products/display-planner.html

Then, use the Display Planner to make a list of five mobile sites or apps where your audience might be on the Display Network.




------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. Social media campaigns for mobile
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Write down the top two social media sites that would be best for your business's advertising efforts, and note the characteristics of your target audience.




------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. Video for mobile
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Create a storyline for a potential mobile video ad. What's your call to action? Remember to keep the mobile video ad short, but relevant.
