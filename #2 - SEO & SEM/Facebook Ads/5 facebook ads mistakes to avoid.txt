-------------------------------------------
    5 Facebook Ad Mistakes to avoid
-------------------------------------------

1. Not having a clear end goal with each Ad

2. Having more than one Call To Action (CTA)
    - Focus on one product on one Ad
    - Sending buyer direct to products page increase buying percentage

3. Sticking to only one type of ad format
    - Try video ads with different forms creatively
    - long or short form
    - Audio or no audio
    - Animated or live action

4. Not taking time to study your competition
    - Use Facebook ad library

5. Making edits too often
    - Make strategy it is very important
